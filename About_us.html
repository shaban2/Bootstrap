About

IDEA literally stands for Innovation Drives Entrepreneurship & Achievement. We purpose to deliberately grow innovation by putting a platform that challenges assumption, ignites thought and questions status quo.

Through entrepreneurship and innovation, IDEA seeks to assume major responsibility for the economic, social and cultural vitality of the communities that surround it

We are promoters of sustainable development in local communities by supporting wealth creation through providing committed partnership, leadership and mentorship offering innovative risk finance solutions for firms that cannot access capital elsewhere.

Ultimately we are a fund with solutions customized for YOU. Driven by our belief in IDEAs and vast sectoral knowledge we power value add for small and medium enterprises (SMEs) by being the missing link.

We deliver this through business platforms and specialist risk financing solutions.

We are passionate about sprouting ideas after all, there is nothing as powerful as an IDEA whose time has come.
