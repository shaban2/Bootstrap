IDEA Crowdsourcing Platform

Do you have an idea? We help you create the World You Want to See, One Idea at a Time.

Our crowdsourcing platform offers an opportunity for ideas to be funded. We are home to changemaking projects, big and small, that are brought to life through the direct support of people like you. Project creators set a funding goal and deadline. If people like a project, they can pledge money to make it happen. It is as simple as;

  • Launching your IDEA.
  • Promoting your IDEA.
  • Considering alternative partnerships for your IDEA.
  • Bringing your IDEA to reality.

Pitch Your IDEA

Have an idea or high-tech startup you want to pitch? Then let’s talk about how your IDEA is a changemaker. Have an opportunity to get feedback others like you, network, and find partners to set you off.

Whether you’re seeking to raise capital, network or cold call, it’s essential that you have a descriptive, concise and well-rehearsed elevator pitch. Come, listen and learn what investors are looking for in an elevator pitch from our team of active investment professionals. The IDEA Pitch is held monthly, enables entrepreneurs to meet our team and have the opportunity to pitch their high-tech idea or startup.

The format is simple, entrepreneurs pitch their ventures and then answer questions and receive feedback from those in attendance. Selected entrepreneurs will make their elevator pitch to members of the IDEA and fellow entrepreneurs in attendance. Following each pitch, the entrepreneur will have the opportunity to address specific questions from the audience and receive pitch-specific feedback. At the conclusion of the pitches, you’ll have the opportunity to network with the IDEA team and fellow entrepreneurs.
